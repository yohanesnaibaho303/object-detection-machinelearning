Opening index.html in your favorite browser should work.

If the samples do not load, try opening Google Chrome by going to Windows > Run and type
chrome.exe  –-allow-file-access-from-files --disable-web-security --user-data-dir --disable-features=CrossSiteDocumentBlockingIfIsolating
OR:
<path to chrome>\chrome.exe  –-allow-file-access-from-files --disable-web-security --user-data-dir --disable-features=CrossSiteDocumentBlockingIfIsolating
Then drag index.html over the browser window.

To test, you may print the samples from print.ppt, or draw your own images, or teach it entirely different things.

Enjoy :-)
